Detailed Description

The queue used for each XG-PON connection at the sender side. Except holding the packets to be transmitted, segmentation is also considered in this class. Hence, we cannot use DropTailQueue directly. Since some variables in ns3::Queue are private, we also cannot inherit from Queue.
