Detailed Description

The class used to instantiate DBA functions of XG-PON at OLT side. It is responsible to interpret queue-status-reports from ONUs and generate BWmap. In another word, it carries out the scheduling of T-CONTs at OLT-side. A simple round-robin scheme is implemented in this sub-class.
