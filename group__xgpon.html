◆ XGPON_XGTC_BW_ALLOCATION_LENGTH

#define XGPON_XGTC_BW_ALLOCATION_LENGTH   8

The per T-CONT bandwidth allocation structure used by XG-PON.

This class has fields corresponding to those in a bandwidth allocation structure used by XG-PON's DBA scheme. This class also includes the methods for serialization to and deserialization from a byte buffer. It follows ITU-T G.987.3 recommendation.
