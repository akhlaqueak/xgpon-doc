void ns3::XgponOnuNetDevice::ProduceAndTransmitUsBurst ( const Ptr< XgponXgtcBwmap > &  map,
uint16_t  first 
)

produce and transmit one upstream burst based on BWmap received previously. this function will be scheduled when ONU processes BWmap through its DBA engine.

Parameters
mapthe BWmap in which this burst is scheduled
firstthe index of the first T-CONT (belonging to this burst) in the BWmap

Framing sub-layer Framing engine will call XGEM engine (for Service-Adaptation sub-layer) and DBA engine directly to fill the payloads. Although we can call Framing, XGEM engine, and upstream scheduler here, it might better to keep XgponOnuNetDevice short.
