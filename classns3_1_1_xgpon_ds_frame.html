Detailed Description

The downstream XGPON frame (XGTC Frame plus PSBd). It is allocated from a pool to avoid too many calls of malloc.

This class has fields corresponding to those in an XGPON downstream frame (the XGTC frame and PSBd) as well as methods for serialization to and deserialization from a byte buffer. It follows ITU-T G.987.3 recommendation.
