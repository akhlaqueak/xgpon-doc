Detailed Description

The class used to instantiate DBA functions of XG-PON at OLT side. It is responsible to interpret queue-status-reports from ONUs and generate BWmap. In another word, it carries out the scheduling of alloc-ids at OLT-side. It is an abstract class and subclasses are implemented for different DBA schemes.
