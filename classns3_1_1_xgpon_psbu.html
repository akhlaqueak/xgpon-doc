Detailed Description

The upstream physical synchronization block the XGPON upstream burst. The length of preamble and delimiter will be determined by the corresponding burst profile.

This class has the corresponding fields as well as methods for serialization to and deserialization from a byte buffer. It follows ITU-T G.987.3 recommendation.
