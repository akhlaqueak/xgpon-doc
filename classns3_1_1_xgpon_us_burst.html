Detailed Description

The upstream XGPON burst (XGTC burst plus PSBu).

This class has fields corresponding to those in an XGPON upstream burst (XGTC burst + PSBu) as well as methods for serialization to and deserialization from a byte buffer. It follows ITU-T G.987.3 recommendation.
